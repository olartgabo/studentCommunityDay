// usage rules · dark vs light surfaces

Dark surfaces (hero, footer, code)

  • Background: navy-700 or ink-900
  • Body text: rgba(255,255,255,0.78)
  • Accent text: cyan-300 · never cyan-400 on dark
  • Borders: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)

Light surfaces (content, agenda, forms)

  • Background: ink-25 / ink-50 · cards ink-0
  • Body text: ink-700 · headings navy-500
  • Accent text: cyan-600 · borders ink-200
  • Focus ring: cyan-400 @ 15% alpha · 3px

10 / Motion

Easing & durations

// confident, not playful

Motion is short, decisive and cubic. Default to 200ms · ease-out. Reserve longer durations (380ms) for entrances. Never bounce. Stagger lists by 40ms increments.
